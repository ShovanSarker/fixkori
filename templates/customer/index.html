How our service works

Just login to our system and send a repair order. We will take care of the rest for you. We take in your request and pass it along to our verified service providers who assesses the situation and schedules a time to come over and repair your equipment. You have the freedom to discuss your service needs and set the price.

The entire service process takes in these few steps:

  • Login or Register to FixKori and place an online order for a supported repair service.
  • We locate a suitable service provider and set up a tentative schedule for his visit.
  • You will be provided with the service provider's contact information and our representative will call you to confirm that the order has been placed.
  • You are now free to communicate with the service provider and arrange the visit schedule according to your convenience.

After you have received your service we would request you to send us a review of the service provider so that we can maintain the quality standard of the service. Your contribution helps greatly to maintaining and upholding the community standards that we hold very dearly.

Features

These are all the features we offer for your convenience!

24x7 availability

All requests are received at any time of the day or night. Our service is open all year round at all times! Service times may vary depending on the availability of the providers, but once ordered, all your requests stay with us and we get back to you as soon as a provider is found!

Price bartering

We love to bargain. It is an integral part of our culture. We allow our users and providers full bargaining capacity in respect to this culture of ours. We can proudly say we are the only service provider who lets you make a bargain :)

Flexible time scheduling

Unable to meet with the provider right now? Feel free to change your schedule to any time you and the provider are comfortable. We do not try to force you in following our rules. Your service, your rules!

Direct communication with service provider

Once a provider has been connected with you, feel free to communicate with him at your own convenience. Feel free to require his services even outside of FixKori. We do not try to control your actions, we try to adjust ourselves to meet your needs!

Pricing

We do not boss you around and tell you what you should pay! We let you take control and dictate your own pricing. We simply bring the service provider to your doorstop!.

Frequently Asked Questions

In case you were wondering, we took the liberty of addressing some of the key confusions.

  • What is the cost of this service?

    We do not define any pricing. We allow you a chance to barter with the service provider for getting the full local flavor of going to the service provider. However, we request you to pay a token amount for the transportation cost of the repairman as a sign of politeness even if you decide not to avail the service. This amount is not mandatory and is communicated between you and the service provider.

  • How long will it take to get connected?

    We usually respond within half an hour of receiving request and connect you with the service provider within that time. After this, you and the provider may choose to set up the schedule according to your convene. However, for late night requests, in case no active service provider is found, we will connect you with the nearest available service provider as soon as he becomes available.

  • Is all repairing done at home?

    This depends on the nature of the issue and between both you and the service provider. If the problem cannot be solved at home, you may choose to allow the service provider to take the equipment to his store to get it repaired and later delivered back to your home, or you may ask to have him bring his equipment and fix it at home. Keep in mind, that some issues cannot be fixed at your doorstop. In those cases, please make sure that you request the service via FixKori so that we are informed of the transaction between you and the provider. This will allow us to intervene and ensure proper care of your equipment. You are free to choose to request service directly from the service provider, but in this case, do understand that any kind of mishap will not be covered by FixKori.
